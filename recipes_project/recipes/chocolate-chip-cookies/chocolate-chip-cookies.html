Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Unsalted butter
  • Granulated sugar
  • Brown sugar
  • Vanilla extract
  • Eggs
  • Chocolate chips

Steps to make

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, beat softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until creamy.
  4. Add vanilla extract and eggs to the butter mixture, beating well.
  5. Gradually add the flour mixture, mixing until combined.
  6. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  7. Drop rounded spoonfuls of dough onto a baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until golden brown.
  9. Let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack.
  10. Enjoy your homemade chocolate chip cookies!
