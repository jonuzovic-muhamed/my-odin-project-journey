Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Pancetta or bacon
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps to make

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add pancetta or bacon and cook until crisp.
  3. Add minced garlic to the skillet and saut√© for about 1 minute.
  4. In a bowl, beat the eggs and mix with grated Parmesan cheese and black pepper.
  5. Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet and toss to combine with the pancetta and garlic.
  6. Remove the skillet from heat and quickly mix in the egg and cheese mixture, stirring until creamy.
  7. Season with salt and more black pepper to taste.
  8. Serve immediately, garnished with extra Parmesan cheese.
