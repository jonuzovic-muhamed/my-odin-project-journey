Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna Ingredients :

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Italian seasoning
  • Fresh basil
  • Egg

Steps to make :

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they are soft.
  4. Add ground beef to the skillet. Cook until browned, breaking it up as it cooks.
  5. Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, salt, black pepper, and Italian seasoning. Simmer for about 15 minutes.
  6. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, and a handful of grated Parmesan cheese. Mix well.
  7. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom.
  8. Place a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  9. Spread a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles.
  10. Sprinkle a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese on top.
  11. Repeat the layers until you run out of ingredients, ending with a layer of meat sauce and a final sprinkling of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  12. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil.
  13. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  14. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  15. Let the lasagna sit for about 10-15 minutes before serving to set.
  16. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and serve. Enjoy!
