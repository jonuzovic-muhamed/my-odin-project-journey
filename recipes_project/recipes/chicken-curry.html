Chicken Curry Recipe

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Curry powder
  • Coconut milk
  • Tomato paste
  • Chicken broth
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Fresh cilantro

Steps to make

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add chopped onion and saut√© until soft.
  2. Add minced garlic and ginger to the pot and cook for another minute.
  3. Add diced chicken breasts and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in curry powder and tomato paste, cooking for another 2 minutes.
  5. Add coconut milk and chicken broth, bringing the mixture to a simmer.
  6. Reduce heat and let the curry simmer for about 20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.
  7. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  8. Serve the chicken curry over rice, garnished with fresh cilantro.
