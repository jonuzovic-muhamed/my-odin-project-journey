Caesar Salad Recipe

Ingredients

  • Romaine lettuce
  • Croutons
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Caesar dressing
  • Chicken breast (optional)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Lemon juice

Steps to make

  1. Chop the Romaine lettuce and place it in a large salad bowl.
  2. If using chicken, season the chicken breast with salt and pepper, and cook in a skillet with olive oil until browned and cooked through. Let it cool and slice into strips.
  3. Add croutons to the salad bowl.
  4. Grate Parmesan cheese over the salad.
  5. Drizzle Caesar dressing over the salad and toss to combine.
  6. Add the sliced chicken breast on top, if using.
  7. Squeeze fresh lemon juice over the salad for extra flavor.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy!
